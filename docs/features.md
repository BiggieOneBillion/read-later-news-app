# Application Features

The **Read Now Or Later News App** is designed for users who want to keep track of interesting articles and news stories without cluttering their bookmarks.

## Core Features

### 1. News Discovery
- Browse the latest news from various sources.
- Responsive grid layout for easy readability.
- Image previews, titles, and snippets for each article.

### 2. "Read Later" Functionality
- Save any article to your personal dashboard with a single click.
- Persistent storage ensures your saved articles are available across sessions.
- Easy removal of articles once you've finished reading them.

### 3. User Dashboard
- A dedicated space to manage all your saved news.
- Sort and view your reading list.
- Direct links to the original source of the news.

### 4. User Authentication
- Secure login and registration.
- Personalized experience where each user has their own unique reading list.
- Protected routes to ensure your data is secure.

## User Flow
1. **Landing Page**: View the latest trending news.
2. **Login/Signup**: Create an account to start saving news.
3. **Save News**: Click the "Save" button on any news card.
4. **Dashboard**: Navigate to your dashboard to see your "Read Later" collection.
5. **Read**: Click on a saved article to go to the source.
6. **Clean Up**: Delete articles from your list once done.
